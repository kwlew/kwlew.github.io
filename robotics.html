Competition Overview

We compete in RoboCup Rescue Maze — a category where fully autonomous robots must navigate an unknown multi-level maze, identify victims, and map the environment in real time without any human input after the run starts. Points are scored for explored tiles, found victims, and successful mapping accuracy.

Our Brazilian qualifier is the CBR (Competição Brasileira de Robótica). In 2025 we swept CBR, winning 1st place overall, Super Teams, Technical Challenge, Team Description Paper, and Video — qualifying us for RoboCup Korea 2026, where we are currently competing.
